During my time with App Innovations I helped design the layout, user interface, and most of the artwork for two digital photo frame apps, PIXSLIDE and FRAMETOGRAPHY. These apps were created to compliment and enhance digital photos taken on mobile devices, with the benefit of instantly posting the results on social media websites.


FRAMETOGRAPHY is aimed for the general public, celebrating special occasions and holidays. While PIXSLIDE is for corporate functions where the frames are customized for a particular event or business.
